## Core Concepts & Frameworks
### Marketing attribution models (last-click, MMM, incrementality)
Frameworks for assigning conversions to influencer touchpoints and measuring true incremental lift.

### Engagement-quality / fraud detection
Methods to detect fake followers, pods and bot engagement vs authentic reach.

### CPM/CPE/CPA & earned-media-value pricing
Standard influencer pricing and value benchmarks for fair sponsorship rates.

### Funnel/AARRR & conversion modeling
Mapping influencer impact across awareness-to-revenue stages.

### FTC disclosure & ad-compliance standards
Required sponsorship disclosure rules affecting compliant campaign design.
